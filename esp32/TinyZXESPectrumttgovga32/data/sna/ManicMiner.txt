Only first 16 characters of this file are used, the remaining are a comment.

The position (index) of the character represents the wiimote key assigned.
idx  value  key
 0 - 0001 - (2)
 1 - 0002 - (1)
 2 - 0004 - trigger
 3 - 0008 - (A)
 4 - 0010 - (-)
 5 - 0020 - N/C
 6 - 0040 - N/C
 7 - 0080 - home
 8 - 0100 - pad left
 9 - 0200 - pad right
 A - 0400 - pad down
 B - 0800 - pad up
 C - 1000 - (+)
 D - 2000 - N/C
 E - 4000 - N/C
 F - 8000 - N/C

The character itself represents the Spectrum key assigned to the wiimote key.

Legal values for Spectrum keys (Spectrum has 40 keys):
1234567890
QWERTYUIOP
ASDFGHJKLe
hZXCVBNMys

e = enter
h = caps shift
y = symbol shift
s = space
